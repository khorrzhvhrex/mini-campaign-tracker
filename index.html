Campaign Tracker — User Guide

Overview

This tool tracks:

  • Army progression
  • Battle results
  • Unit survival
  • Marked for Greatness awards
  • Unit notes and datasheets
  • Campaign save/load

Getting Started

Rename Armies: Click army names.

Select Banner Theme: Use color selector.

Add Units: Use Add Unit.

Unit Reference

Click Unit Name 📜 for notes.

Click Unit Type ⚔ for datasheet.

Recording a Battle

Use Record Victory for the winning army.

Required each mission:

  • 1 MFG for Army A
  • 1 MFG for Army B

Heart of the Void Points

At 3, 5, and 6 HV Points, skulls become stars.

Click ★ to spend HV points for additional MFG.

Survival Rewards

  • 1 Survival → +1 XP
  • 2 Survivals → +3 XP
  • 3 Survivals → Blooded

Marked for Greatness

Units gain HEART OF THE WAR

Allows re-roll failed Out of Action tests.

Note: IF a HEART OF THE WAR unit is removed from your Order of Battle in the Grand Campaign, your opponent gains D3 Command Points at the start of the next X battles, with X equal to the number of H.O.T.W. units removed. This effect is cumulative.

Example: Player A removes 2 H.O.T.W. units after Battle 1. Player B gains D3 CP at the start of Battle 2 and 3. Player A removes 2 more H.O.T.W. units after Battle 2. Player B gains D3 CP at the start of Battle 3, 4, and 5.
